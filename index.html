How To Use This Page

A fast workflow for pre-call prep, discovery follow-up, and objection handling.

Step 1

Select the competitors in your evaluation

Use the roster below to choose the vendors the prospect is evaluating. The battlecard automatically compares them against Backblaze Computer Backup.

Step 2

Review pricing, scope, and limits

Focus on what matters in a sales conversation: per-device vs pooled storage, restore paths, retention, and where a competitor requires a higher tier to match core expectations.

Step 3

Export the battlecard for the team

Export as text or PDF and drop it into the account brief, enablement doc, or Slack thread so the sales team uses one clear positioning story.

Why You Should Use It

The page is designed to make competitive positioning faster and more concrete.

Clarify tradeoffs

Show when “unlimited” means one computer, one user, or a pooled account with storage caps elsewhere.

Reduce vague talk tracks

Replace generic positioning with specific restore, retention, security, and plan-limit details.

Speed up prep

Get to a usable comparison in a few clicks instead of rebuilding notes in every deal cycle.
